## 常数

$$
(C)\prime = 0
$$

## 三角函数XXX

$$
(sinx)\prime = cosx
\\
(cosx)\prime = -sinx
\\
(tanx)\prime = (secx) ^ 2
\\
$$

## 幂函数

$$
(x ^ n)\prime = nx ^ {n - 1}
$$

## 指数函数

$$
(a ^ x)\prime = a ^ x \ast \ln^{a}
\\
(e ^ x)\prime = e ^ x
\\
$$

## 对数函数

$$
(\log_a^{x})\prime = \frac{1}{x\ln^a}
\\
(\ln^{x})\prime = \frac{1}{x}
$$
