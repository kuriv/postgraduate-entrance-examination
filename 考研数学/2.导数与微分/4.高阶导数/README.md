# 高阶导数

$$
(sinx) ^ {(n)} = sin(x + n \ast \frac{\pi}{2})
\\
(cosx) ^ {(n)} = cos(x + n \ast \frac{\pi}{2})
\\
[\ln^{(x + 1)}] ^ {(n)} = (-1) ^ {n - 1} \ast \frac{(n - 1)!}{(x + 1) ^ n}
\\
(u \pm v) ^ {(n)} = u ^ {(n)} \pm v ^ {(n)}
\\
(uv) ^ {(n)} = {\Sigma_{k = 0} ^ n}{C_n ^ k}{u ^ {(k)}}{v ^ {(n - k)}}
$$
